Shepherd's Pie

Description:

This bacon carbonara is a super rich, classic bacon and egg spaghetti dish that's great to serve for company

This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed
  • 8 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients

    • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
    • Cook spaghetti, stirring occasionally, until al dente (about 12 minutes)
    • Drain and toss with olive oil, set aside

    • Place diced bacon in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until evenly browned
    • Drain bacon on paper towels, reserving 2 tablespoons of bacon fat in the skillet

    • Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to bacon fat in the skillet
    • Add chopped onion and cook over medium heat until translucent
    • Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute
    • Add wine and cook 1 minute more

    • Return cooked bacon to the skillet, and add the spaghetti
    • Toss to coat and heat through, adding more olive oil if sticky or dry
    • Add beaten eggs and cook, tossing constantly with tongs until eggs are barely set
    • Quickly add 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese and toss again
    • Season to taste with salt and pepper (heads up: bacon and Parmesan are very salty)

  6. Serve warm with chopped parsley and a bit more Parmesan sprinkled on top
